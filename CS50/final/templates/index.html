Test Your Strength!


The 9c Strength test is a Strength test developed by Norwegian climbers, Stian Christopherson and Martin Mobraten. The test measures a climber's strength in 4 areas; raw pull up power, crimp strength, core strength and an endurance hang. For each test points are assigned depending on the performance, these points can be used to guestimate your climbing grade, or just as a benchmark for your training. Watch the video below for more.
